About us

We are trusted all across Hampton Roads

TRACº Specialty Inc. has been dedicated to serving the residential and commercial sector of Hampton Roads since 1992. Founded with just a toolbox, a truck, and knowledge of the trade by Tony and Sheila Atkins, our business has built a superior reputation of not just superb workmanship, but of exceptional craftsmanship through word of mouth by our loyal customers. Refrigeration and HVAC service and repair is not just a profession to us, it is what we enjoy and love. As a result, every job receives our full respect, care, and concern. The mission of TRACº Specialty Inc. is to satisfy all of your commercial refrigeration and HVAC needs with complete satisfaction and High Degree Performance every time.

TRACº Specialty Inc. offers a variety of services that will cater to all commercial needs – restaurants, offices, retail, and more. We offer competitive pricing for unit replacement, service, and preventative maintenance programs.
