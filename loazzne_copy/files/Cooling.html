Should I Repair or Replace My Air Conditioner?

  • Age - If your AC is more than 10 years old and giving you trouble, it may be in your best interest of consider a replacement.
  • Investment cost - Is a new system a better investment in the long run? One of our technicians can help you calculate your return on investment to determine if on upgrade in more beneficial than repair.
  • Utility rates - You may think you can't afford an air conditioner replacement, but a more efficient unit consumes less electricity and could save you big time on energy bills.
